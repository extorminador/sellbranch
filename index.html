Fredrik Sellgren - CEO and Founder

“Quisque lacinia volutpat lectus, vel egestas dolor imperdiet”

Fredrik Sellgren is a seasoned Internet professional having lead the international expansion of the Tradedoubler ad network as well as having been the MD for TradeDoubler Sweden. Fredrik was the CEO of Keynote Media Group, the digital investment house that held companies such as Spray, Passagen, Nöjesguiden and Rodeo.

Fredrik is now the founder and CEO of Sellbranch, one of the largest Programmatic players in the Nordics. Sellbranch helps about 60 larger publisher with their sales and programmatic strategy.

Ola Ahlvarsson - Founder

“Quisque lacinia volutpat lectus, vel egestas dolor imperdiet”

Ola Ahlvarsson is one of the pioneers of the European Internet industry as a founder of Result and as a co-founder or expansion partner of a growing number of other Internet and technology related businesses.

Ola has been named leading Internet entrepreneur by Wall street journal and serves on the selection committee of the World Economic forums technology pioneer program as well as the jury of Le Web and Media Momentum.

Ola is the chairman and main share holder of SIME, northern Europe’s largest event about digital opportunities and business.
