About

Sellbranch was founded 2009 by Tradedoubler’s former MD Fredrik Sellgren and Internet entrepreneur Ola Ahlvarsson.

The company was started with the vision to become a Gateway to the Nordics for the latest online technologies and to help publisher increase revenue. 

2009

Sellbranch is started as a digital sales house and Yeald Optimisation Partner for Publishers. Keynote Media Group Invest in Sellbranch. Sellbranch representing publishers such as Miniclip, Spray and many more.

2010

Sellbranch launchd partnership with Yahoo! Mini clip Spray and many more sites cross nordics.

2013

Sellbranch signes partnership with Improve digital and lanuged a managed SSP (supplly side platform) to publishers.

2014

Swisscom invest in Sellbranch. Sellbranch wins Twitter representation in the Nordics. Sellbranch opens office in Norway.

2015

Sellbranch Double revenu on 2015 and goes from 11 to 20 people.

Direct Sales

Twitter

Sellbranch are the exclusive partner representing Twitter in the Nordics. Twitter are one of the worlds largest social media platforms and influence people every day. In the nordics Twitter reach millions of users on a daily basis. Sellbranch assignment is predominantly to increase advertising revenue buy helping advertisers and agencies buy promoted Accoutns, promoted Tweets and promoted Trends.

Yahoo!

Yahoo is one five global platforms that reached over 1 billion users every month. Sellbranch are the exclusive representative of Yahoo in the nordic region. The assignment includes helping selling Native, programatic and various display ads on Yahoo platfomrms such as Yahoo.se, Yahoo.com, Tumblr, Flickr, flurry etc.

Others

Sellbranch help several other publishers and platforms to generate revenue. This includes Nordics lagerst cashual gaming platform Spilgames, Mail and contentprotal Spray.se, mobile ad network Leadmill and more.
